Homeowners looking to save money while embracing renewable energy can benefit from the Residential Clean Energy Credit offered by the IRS. This credit covers a portion of the costs associated with installing clean energy systems like solar panels, wind turbines, geothermal heat pumps, fuel cells, or battery storage technology in their homes.

The credit, available until 2034, provides a significant financial incentive, offsetting up to 30% of the expenses incurred. (Do note, tax credit is NOT refundable, but can be carried over for later tax years.)
