Smart Homes

The open-source standard "Matter" is gaining traction as the go-to solution for integrating smart devices into IoT (Internet of Things) ecosystems, promising seamless control of various aspects of a smart home under one unified system. Amazon is leveraging AI through Amazon Alexa to enhance automation, optimizing energy usage for lights, thermostats, and other appliances, thereby alleviating the hassle of manual setup and promoting eco-friendly practices. With the ongoing evolution of the industry, the initial cost premium for energy-efficient and smart appliances is decreasing, making it an opportune moment to embrace sustainability in smart home technology and reap financial savings.

Smart Lighting

Many smart light bulbs now integrate with Matter, allowing users to control brightness and color temperature effortlessly from their smartphones.

Climate Control

Smart thermostats compatible with Matter can adjust temperature settings based on occupancy patterns, saving energy and ensuring comfort.

Appliance Optimization

With the use of AI, Amazon Alexa can adjust smart appliances according to users' preferences, optimizing energy usage without sacrificing convenience.

Refrigeration

Smart refrigerators with Matter integration can monitor food, suggest recipes, and adjust cooling settings to minimize energy consumption while making grabbing a snack easier than ever.
